How does this work?

CentralSearch provides one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more. Sign in to:

  • Access subscription resources (databases, ebooks, ejournals, online articles)
  • View library loans & requests
  • Renew & request library materials
  • Create favorites lists & export citations
